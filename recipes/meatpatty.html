A meat patty (kotlet mielony)

Kotlet mielony

Kotlet mielony is a meat patty which is paired with potatoes or fries, preferably potatoes.

You can eat it with with a bunch of salads but I recomennd just beetroot.

    Ingredients

  • You can use every meat: pork, beef or chicken. We're gonna use beef
  • 1 egg
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper

    Steps to make it

  1. Grind meat
  2. Season it with salt and pepper
  3. Cover it in an egg, then in flour
  4. Fry it on cooking oil
