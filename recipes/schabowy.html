A pork chop

A pork chop with chicken meat

It is a traditional polish dish which is basically a 'chop with a chicken breast' but we call it a pork chop as well

It is basically a fried piece of meat and you can eat it with everything but most people eat it with potatoes

    Ingredients

  • Pork or chicken meat
  • 1 egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or cooking oil

    Steps to make it

  1. Beat the meat lightly
  2. Season it with salt and pepper
  3. Cover it in bread crumbs, then in an egg and again bread crumbs
  4. Get a frying pan hot and put cooking oil or butter in
  5. Place the meat inside a frying pan
