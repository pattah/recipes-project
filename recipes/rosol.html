Polish chicken soup

It is a must have if you visit Poland. One of the most popular dishes in the country.

Chicken broth with noodles and herbs, that's what rosol is. If you taste it when it's hot, it is one of the best dishes in the world and how simple it is to make.

    Ingredients

  • A few carrots
  • Noodles, thin ones
  • Chicken broth
  • Salt, pepper and herbs

    Steps to make it

  1. Chop up carrots and herbs
  2. Start boiling chicken broth
  3. Season the chicken broth with herbs, salt and pepper
  4. Cook pasta on the side
  5. That's it! The dish is ready!
